# 📖 VoteSecure — Student User Guide

> Step-by-step instructions for CMP 300 Level students

---

## Step 1 — Open the Voting System

Ask your Course Representative or class coordinator for the link or the `.html` file.  
Open it in any modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari).

You will see the **VoteSecure** landing page with three options.

---

## Step 2 — Create Your Account (First Time Only)

1. Click **📝 Create Account**
2. Fill in all three fields:

   | Field | What to enter | Example |
   |---|---|---|
   | Full Name | First and last name | `Muhammad Yusuf Madaki` |
   | Matric Number | Your university matric | `FT23CMP0042` |
   | Email | Your university email | `ft23cmp042@univ.edu.ng` |

3. The matric number auto-converts to uppercase as you type
4. Green ✅ indicators appear when each field is valid
5. Click **✅ Create My Account**
6. You will be automatically redirected to the Login page

> ⚠️ You only need to register **once**. If you see "Matric No already registered", go to Login instead.

---

## Step 3 — Log In

1. Click **🔐 Student Login** (or you were redirected here after sign-up)
2. Enter your **Matriculation Number** (e.g. `FT23CMP0042`)
3. Press **Enter** or click **Access Voter Portal →**

---

## Step 4 — Choose Your Candidate

You will see the Voter Portal with all candidates listed.

- Each card shows the candidate's **name**, **position**, and a **short manifesto preview**
- Click **▼ Read full manifesto** to expand and read their complete message
- Click a candidate's card to **select** them — a teal border and ✓ will appear
- Click the same card again to **deselect**

---

## Step 5 — Submit Your Vote

1. Once a candidate is selected, the **🗳️ Submit My Vote** button becomes active
2. Click it — a confirmation modal will appear
3. Review your choice carefully
4. Click **✅ Confirm Vote** to cast your vote permanently

> ⚠️ **This cannot be undone.** You may only vote once.

---

## Step 6 — Save Your Receipt

After voting, you will receive a **Transaction Receipt ID** like:

```
TXN-AB1C2D-XYZ789
```

- **Screenshot or write this down** — it proves your vote was counted
- Your receipt ID is NOT linked to your choice — your vote remains anonymous
- Click **Close** to return to the voter portal

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I forgot my matric number format — what should it look like?**  
A: `FT23CMP` followed by a 4-digit number from `0001` to `0500`. Example: `FT23CMP0042`.

**Q: I entered my matric number but it says "not found".**  
A: You haven't registered yet. Go back to the landing page and click "Create Account" first.

**Q: I already voted but want to change my choice.**  
A: That is not possible. Votes are permanent and cannot be changed.

**Q: Is my vote really secret?**  
A: Yes. Your name and matric number are **never stored alongside your vote**. Only an anonymous receipt ID is recorded.

**Q: The page says "Election Closed". Can I still vote?**  
A: No. Once the election is closed by the Course Representative, no further votes can be cast.
